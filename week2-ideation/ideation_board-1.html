4
Key Research Insights
From 28 survey responses collected during Week 1
78%

Lost at least once

Of students reported being late or confused when finding a new location on campus.

91%

Use mobile phones

Would primarily use a navigation app on their Android or iPhone on campus.

84%

Want offline access

Said campus WiFi is unreliable so offline navigation is very important.

#1

Biggest pain point

No proper signboards or maps available on campus — voted top challenge.

96%

Would use the app

Said they would "definitely" or "probably" use a Smart Campus Navigation app.
